Rules and regulations

Category - For all 


  • Each student takes part in the event as an individual participant. The event is divided into 3 rounds. After every round, the names of the qualifying participants will be announced and they will then move to the next round.

  • The first round is an extempore round where each participant gets a minute to prepare and a minute to advocate unpopular views in support of a particular and publicly disputed topic.

  • The second round is a Group Discussion that involves selected students teaming up and discussing a certain topic on the floor.

  • The final round consists of the Asian Parliamentary format of debating which includes speech and rebuttal by each participant justifying the side he/she is on.


    • Note: The participants should join the google meet link provided to them 20 minutes before each round. The videos of the participants should be on throughout and they should have a stable internet connection.

    #StriveToThrive

    When words fail, art, in all of its forms, steps in. We live in a country where the entertainment industry is thriving, but in the midst of it all, we must remember that we are not individuals, but members of a larger society and our responsibility to that society must not be overlooked. KGT is a forum where individuals may express themselves via music, dance, theatre, and creative writing while keeping our society and our responsibilities to it in mind.

    Theme

    Participants who want to showcase their skills should remember that their performance/write-ups should be focused on the following topic :

  • "She's a muse to herself".Feminism is the conviction that women should have complete social, economic, and political equality. Let the women of our society strive and flourish.
  • "Child abuse leaves a lifetime's worth of scars". Abusing a child is as terrible as murdering a person because children are naturally vulnerable, and whatever abuse they experience in their early years leaves a permanent mark on their minds. Let us strive for a better world for the children.
  • "Making someone else appear awful is never a good look". Let's bring an end to bullying.
  • "By helping others, we may soar". Helping others is not only beneficial to them and a noble act, but it also makes us happier and healthier. Let us all strive to be happy and healthy.
  • "Let our hearts be overflowing with gratitude and generosity".
  • "You will smile once again". The mind is one of the body's most powerful organs, controlling the functions of all other organs. It is critical for everyone to strive for a balance between mental and physical well-being and to seek assistance when either falters.
  • "Go green to breathe clean".It is only feasible to live a healthy life if the basic demand of clean air and land is provided. A pollution-free and green environment equates to a higher level of life.

    Rules and regulations:

  • DANCE: Participants should submit a video of themselves dancing. The video should be no more than three minutes long. The outfit, props, and performances of the participants will be evaluated. The dance should be relevant to the issues mentioned above.
  • SONG: Participants should record themselves singing and submit it as a video. The video should not go more than three minutes. The song can be self-written and connected to the above-mentioned issues. Participants can sing while playing an instrument or instruments.
  • WRITE-UP: On the above-mentioned subjects, participants can produce short stories, personal essays, poetry, short plays, memoirs, and speeches. Participants must submit their writing as a pdf file. The writing should be original and not plagiarised from other sources.
  • THEATRE: Participants can act in a skit, monologue, or any other type of performance. They must record the entire performance on video. The video should be no longer than 7-8 minutes in length. The performance should be related to the above-mentioned topics.
  • Participation: Solo, Duet, Trio, and Teams (no size limit for teams).
  • Use of fire, water, and any obscene object or portraying indecency in any form will straight away lead to disqualification.
  • Judging Criteria: Creativity, Technique, Stage Presence, connection with the audience and portrayal of theme, Props usage.
  • A person is bound to stop the performance midway if directed by the judge.
  • The judge’s decision is final and no acquisitions or queries shall be entertained.
  • Props and instruments need to be arranged by the performers.

    Note: The videos or pdfs should be sent in a zip file in the given link on the day of the event. The name of the file should be team/participant name_domain. For example, xyz_dance.
